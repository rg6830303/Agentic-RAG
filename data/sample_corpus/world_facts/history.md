# World History Highlights

## Ancient civilizations
- **Mesopotamia** (c. 3500 BCE): cradle of urban civilization, cuneiform writing, the Code of Hammurabi.
- **Ancient Egypt**: unified c. 3100 BCE under Narmer; pyramids built during the Old Kingdom (c. 2700–2200 BCE).
- **Indus Valley Civilization**: planned cities of Harappa and Mohenjo-Daro (c. 2600–1900 BCE).
- **Ancient Greece**: democracy emerged in Athens (5th century BCE); philosophy from Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle.
- **Roman Republic and Empire**: Republic founded 509 BCE; Empire began 27 BCE under Augustus; Western collapse 476 CE.

## Middle Ages
- **Fall of the Western Roman Empire** (476 CE) is the conventional start.
- **Islamic Golden Age** (8th–13th centuries): mathematics, medicine, astronomy preserved and advanced in Baghdad's House of Wisdom.
- **Tang and Song dynasties** in China invented printing, gunpowder, and the compass.
- **Renaissance** began in 14th-century Italy, spreading classical learning across Europe.

## Early modern era
- **Columbian Exchange** (1492 onwards): transcontinental movement of plants, animals, people, and disease following Columbus's voyages.
- **Scientific Revolution** (16th–17th centuries): Copernicus, Galileo, Kepler, and Newton reframed physics and astronomy.
- **Industrial Revolution** began in Britain c. 1760, then spread; steam power, mechanized textiles, factory system.

## 20th century
- **World War I** (1914–1918) and **World War II** (1939–1945) reshaped global powers.
- **Cold War** (1947–1991) split the world between US-led and Soviet-led blocs.
- **Decolonization** swept Asia and Africa, especially 1945–1975. India became independent in 1947.
- **Internet emerged** from ARPANET in 1969; the World Wide Web was invented by Tim Berners-Lee at CERN in 1989.

## 21st century landmarks
- **September 11, 2001** attacks reshaped US foreign policy.
- **Smartphones go mainstream** after the 2007 iPhone launch.
- **2008 global financial crisis** triggered the Great Recession.
- **COVID-19 pandemic** (declared March 2020) caused worldwide lockdowns and accelerated remote work.
- **Generative AI boom** (2022–): ChatGPT launches November 2022, sparking a wave of LLM products.
