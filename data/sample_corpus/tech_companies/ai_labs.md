# Major AI Research Labs

## OpenAI
OpenAI is an American AI research and deployment company founded in December 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, John Schulman, and Wojciech Zaremba. Its mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity. Sam Altman is the CEO. OpenAI launched ChatGPT in November 2022, which reached 100 million users in two months. Major products: the GPT family of models (GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4o), the DALL·E image models, Sora video model, and the o-series reasoning models.

## Anthropic
Anthropic is an American AI safety and research company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei, along with several former OpenAI researchers. Dario Amodei is the CEO and Daniela Amodei is the President. The company focuses on building reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. Its flagship product is the Claude family of large language models, with Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku tiers. Anthropic's research areas include Constitutional AI, mechanistic interpretability, and scalable oversight.

## Google DeepMind
Google DeepMind is an AI research laboratory based in London, formed in 2023 by merging DeepMind (founded 2010 by Demis Hassabis, Shane Legg, and Mustafa Suleyman) and Google Brain. Demis Hassabis is the CEO. Notable accomplishments include AlphaGo, AlphaFold (protein structure prediction), Gemini models, and reinforcement learning breakthroughs.

## Meta AI / FAIR
Meta AI is the artificial intelligence research division of Meta Platforms, including the Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) group founded in 2013 under Yann LeCun, who serves as Chief AI Scientist. Meta released the Llama family of open-weight LLMs starting in 2023, and the Segment Anything Model (SAM) for computer vision.

## Mistral AI
Mistral AI is a French AI startup founded in April 2023 by Arthur Mensch (CEO), Guillaume Lample, and Timothée Lacroix, three researchers formerly at Google DeepMind and Meta. The company is known for open-weight efficient LLMs such as Mistral 7B, Mixtral, and Mistral Large.

## xAI
xAI is an American AI company founded by Elon Musk in 2023. Its flagship product is the Grok family of LLMs.
