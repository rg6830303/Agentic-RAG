# Ray Optics and Optical Instruments

Reflection follows the law that angle of incidence equals angle of reflection. Refraction occurs when light changes medium, causing a change in speed and direction.

For lenses, the thin lens formula is written as 1/f = 1/v - 1/u using the Cartesian sign convention. A convex lens can form real or virtual images depending on object position.

Magnifying power describes how much larger an object appears compared with direct viewing. Optical instruments like microscopes and telescopes combine lenses to control image formation and angular magnification.
